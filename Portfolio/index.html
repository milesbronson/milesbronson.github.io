Research

Bipedal Robotics

Working with Prof. Alex Halderman, working with reinforcement learning to model and train a robot using Frost and Mathlab. Lab involved teaching underclassmen underlying physics.

Occalating Gel Robotics

Alongside Profs. Mark Newman and Elizabeth Bruch at U of M, built a Markovian model to analyse academic pathways/curricular environments. Worked with large-scale course enrollment data (more than 1 million rows) and interweaved sociology, network theory, mathematics, and computer science.


Recent Projects

Google Search Engine

Created a PageRank-based reputation ranking system, used Hadoop Streaming to create a segmented inverted index, and created a distributed system for search. Results were accurate and in my very biased opinion a better UI.

Wordle Bot

Created a bot for the popular word game Wordle using Machine Learning. Specifically, we use decision trees and entropy to narrow the ”space” of possible guesses every iteration. The bot beats the game 100% of the time!

N- Particle Simulator

Developed a physics simulator that takes in an initial configuration of particles and models their motion based on their respective masses. Optimized with Leap-Frog simulation algorithm.
